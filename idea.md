Live Geopolitical Risk Dashboard - full stack web-app with a 3D rotating globe, powered by Firecrawl API scrapes breaking news, oil prices, flight radar blogs, and defense stock tickers in real-time. One screen showing a global threat map with color-coded risk levels that auto-update.

Interactive Drill-Downs: Let users click on a country to see a popup/sidebar explaining why it has a certain risk level (showing the specific news headline, flight data, or stock movement that triggered it).

Timeline Slider: Add a historical playback slider so users can see how global risk has evolved over the past week/month.


Custom Data Layers: Add toggles so users can choose to view only news sentiment, only defense stocks, or the combined threat level on the globe.

Search & Quick Zoom: A search bar to instantly fly/zoom the 3D globe to a specific country or region.
